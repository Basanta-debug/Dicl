Mission

To produce Iron and steel based on the iron deposit of Nepal ,especially on Dhaubadi-Iron deposit, to meet the domestic demand of steel and to increase economic growth by reducing the major proportion of steel ingot.


Vision

To be a leading institution from Nepal in steel business in terms of quality, and customer satisfaction.


Our Smart Aproach

  • The company is committed to establish the iron and steel plant with the latest technology that has a less environmental impact on the surrounding.

  • The success of the company not only to utilize the natural resources but largely contributes to the socio-economic development of the country.

  • It will reduce major proportion of imported steel and consume small/medium iron deposit of the country.

  • At the same time promotion of such industries leads the Nation towards industrialization and economic growth.

  • It will create job opportunities, helps the allied industries that will ultimately help to the growth of national Gross Development Product (GDP).


Objectives

Establish a steel plant with the production of high-quality iron and reduce the import of Iron through Public-Private Partnership Model using its own iron ore.


  • Invite both domestic and foreign investors on the project.

  • Explore the Iron Deposit around the Dhaubadi area of Nawalparasi for the commercial production of iron with modern technology in a friendly environment.

  • Develop technical manpower to collaborate with national and international experts. Develop skilled human resources in-country.

  • Explore other potential iron and other minerals in Nepal.

  • To produce high-quality steel from its natural resources found in-country
