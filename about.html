About Us

Dhaubadi Iron Company Limited (DICL) is established in B.S. 2076 and intends to produce iron and steel from iron ore deposit located in Nawalparasi East, Nepal. Presently, DICL has a office at Lainchaur, Kathmandu with Geologist, Mining engineers, Metallurgical Engineer, chartered Accountant and administrative manpower with required infrastructure and is planning to set-up a site office at Kawasoti, Nawalparasi East, in near future.

DICL is conducting the detailed exploration work of mines, metallurgical pilot testing for extracting the iron.Iron, steel groups represent the highest dollar value in Nepal’s import purchases during 2019. Also which is about $983.8 million (10.2%). Dhaubadi Iron Company Limited (DICL) intends to produce iron and steel in near future. It is conducting exploration works in the iron mineralization deposit in the Hupsekot Rural Municipality-5, Nawalparasi East, Gandaki Province, Nepal. This area lies about 20km north from East-West Highway. The highest policy making and controlling body of DICL is its Board of Directors. Chief executive director of DICL is member secretary and is responsible for management and operations. DICL has implemented guideline/act.
